2.2 Population vs. Sample

Key Concepts

  • Population: Entire group of interest for a study
  • Sample: A specified part of the population
  • Census: Official count of entire population with detailed information

Statistical Terms

  • Outliers
  • Range
  • Representative Sample
  • Destructive Study

Full Population Example

Population Size: 50 students

Attribute to Check: Allergies

Approach: Check allergies for ALL 50 students

Reason: Need comprehensive health information

Sample Population Example

Total Population: 50 students

Sample Size: 10 students

Attribute to Check: Soda preferences for party

Reason: Limited variation in soda preferences

Method: Sampling can provide sufficient insight
