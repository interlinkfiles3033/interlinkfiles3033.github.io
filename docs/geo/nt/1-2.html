Probability to Odds Formula

Odds in favor of A = P(A) / P(not A)

Converting probability into actionable insights

Dice Roll Probability Example

Sample Space: {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}

Event: Odds against rolling a 5 or 6

Calculation: (4/6) / (2/6) = 2:1

Interpretation: 2 to 1 odds against rolling a 5 or 6
